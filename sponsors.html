The USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Club is inviting you to support us in our activities!

Sponsoring the USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Club is an excellent opportunity for your firm to introduce itself and to network with USC students with a strong interest in the private equity and venture capital industry. By partnering with our club you can gain access to our current members, USC alumni working in the private equity industry, as well as more than one million readers of our publications. The USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Club is one of the largest and most active professional clubs at USC. Our 800+ members include students with prior PE/VC experience as well as those interested in the industry.

The Club provides a host of sponsorship opportunities:

  • USC PE & VCC Speaker Series – World-class speakers at USC lecturing to undergraduate and graduate future business leaders of tomorrow
  • USC Private Equity Review – Annual publication reaching more than 1 million readers globally, including USC students, alumni and industry professionals
  • USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Conference – The preeminent student organized industry forum of its kind, bringing together prominent GPs, LPs and other industry professionals with USC alumni, faculty and students. Event covered by most major media outlets covering the private equity and venture capital industry
  • Case Competitions – Several case competitions attracting students across the top 20 global MBA programs and a large number of PE/VC professionals
  • Additional Sponsorship Opportunities – We can offer you prominent branding in our regular communications, or feature your company professionals at our speaker series, member educational workshops, and recruiting activities

If you are interested in supporting us and would like to discuss further, please reach out to Andrew Bentley (ahbentle@usc.edu).
