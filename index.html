Overview

Club Mission

The University of Southern California PEVC Club is committed to providing its members with career and educational opportunities in private equity and venture capital. We provide a platform for students with interest in private equity and venture capital and can meet and network amongst each other but also with leading industry professionals in the field. Our events are targeted both at students with prior experience in the industry and at students planning to devote themselves in finance industries following graduation. The club members will learn how to find, select and operate private equity and venture capital, and setup scientific corporate expansion strategy through M&A to elevate company value through Pre-IPO or VC...etc. We will equipped our members with the essential knowledge of major financial theories, calculations and simulate the real scenario via real life experiences to learn global integration and leadership skills. We will held conferences, distinguished speeches, educational information sessions, social network events and formalizing job hiring resume books to continuously improving and maintaining USC PEVC Club, to exceed the excellence.

Field Application Projects

A program designed to integrate years of advanced study by Marshall students, as they apply what they have learned to the problems of organizations in the community. The project begins with students making project selections in November. The course runs through mid-April. During this time, the student teams devote a total of approximately 200 hours conducting research and analysis in order to research a problem facing the host company. Each team is supervised by experienced Marshall faculties and closely mentored by a faculty appointed teaching assistant. The teams consist of 1-2 teams per project with 4-6 students per team. The project culminates in a detailed written analysis with action recommendations.

PEVC Conference

The annual USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Conference is one of the largest of its kind in the world and attracts leaders from across the industry and provides a number of keynote speeches and panel discussions.

Keynote and Panel topics will focus on issues such as the state of the industry and prospects for the future, fundraising environment, value creation opportunities and challenges, understanding and adapting to new regulations, and delivering superior returns in uncertain market conditions. We are honored to feature a distinguished group of Keynote and Panel speakers who will contribute their time and insight for the benefit of the audience. The conference offers attendees a unique opportunity for learning and networking with others interested in thoughtful discourse on these key topics and on the industry as a whole.

USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Association

USC Private Equity & Venture Capital Association is open to private equity investment professionals who have graduated from The USC School or the University of Pennsylvania AND who are investing third party capital. In addition to being a USC or UPenn alum, to be eligible for membership, prospective members must also be professionally investing in venture capital, growth capital, mezzanine capital, buyouts, or secondaries as a general partner. Alumni are also eligible if they are limited partners of funds or are professionals managing third party fund-of-funds targeting the private equity space. To learn more about the organization and events organized, please go to www.wpen.org.
