  • WHY PEVC?

    We are committed to providing our members with prevailing knowledge in the field and practical on-site experiences. Members of the club will be able to meet and network with industry professionals and explore opportunities through our events.

  • SYMPOSIUM

    Our annual symposium invites industry leaders across the country and provides keynote speeches and panel discussions. The attendees will receive unique opportunities to learn from successful entrepreneurs and market specialists who contribute their time and insights on key topics of the industry.

  • COMPETITION

    The Business Plan Competition is designed to give entrepreneurs and innovators from around the world a chance to pitch in front of VC and PE firms and an opportunity to fund their products. We provide a valuable experience for participants to interact and receive feedbacks from the panel of judges.
