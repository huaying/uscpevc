Club Mission

The University of Southern California PEVC Club is committed to providing its members career and educational opportunities in private equity and venture capital firms. We offer a platform for students interested in private equity and venture capital to meet and network amongst each other and also for them to meet with leading industry professionals. Our events are designed for both students with prior experience and those who plan to devote themselves to finance careers following graduation.

The club members will learn how to locate, analyze and select private equity and/or venture capital firms. We strive to equip our members with the essential knowledge of major financial theories, calculations and leadership skills. We hold conferences, distinguished speeches, educational information sessions and social network events. Explore your potential and become a part of us through following us on Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn. We hope to see you soon at our events.

Club Activities

Club activities can be broadly classified into four categories:

BEING INFORMED (Information)

  • Regular update emails
  • Access to proprietary resources
  • USC Private Equity Review
  • USC Private Equity Recruiting Guide

GETTING HIRED (Jobs)

  • Job postings
  • Career Treks (to most key worldwide locations)
  • Interview preparation
  • Club resume book
  • Mentoring program

PREPARING FOR THE JOB (Education)

  • USC Private Equity Conference
  • PE&VC Club Education Series & Certificate
  • Speaker series
  • Competitions(Stay Tuned)
  • Peer industry panels and coffee chats

HAVING FUN AND NETWORKING (Social)

  • Club socials
  • Mixers
  • Breakfast/lunch series
