Club Mission

The University of Southern California PEVC Club is committed to providing its members with career and educational opportunities in private equity and venture capital. We provide a platform for students with interest in private equity and venture capital and can meet and network amongst each other but also with leading industry professionals in the field. Our events are targeted both at students with prior experience in the industry and at students planning to devote themselves in finance industries following graduation. The club members will learn how to find, select and operate private equity

and venture capital, and setup scientific corporate expansion strategy through M&A to elevate company value through Pre-IPO or VC...etc. We will equipped our members with the essential knowledge of major financial theories, calculations and simulate the real scenario via real life experiences to learn global integration and leadership skills. We will held conferences, distinguished speeches, educational information sessions, social network events and formalizing job hiring resume books to continuously improving and maintaining USC PEVC Club, to exceed the excellence.

Club Activities

Club activities can be broadly classified into four categories:

BEING INFORMED (Information)

  1. Regular update emails
  2. Access to proprietary resources
  3. USC Private Equity Review
  4. USC Private Equity Recruiting Guide

GETTING HIRED (Jobs)

  1. Job postings
  2. Career Treks (to most key worldwide locations)
  3. Interview preparation
  4. Club resume book
  5. Mentoring program

PREPARING FOR THE JOB (Education)

  1. USC Private Equity Conference
  2. PE&VC Club Education Series & Certificate
  3. Speaker series
  4. Competitions(Stay Tuned)
  5. Peer industry panels and coffee chats

HAVING FUN AND NETWORKING (Social)

  1. Club socials
  2. Mixers
  3. Breakfast/lunch series
